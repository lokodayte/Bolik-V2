About UWC Dilijan

Where education meets purpose

🌍

United World Colleges

UWC is a global education movement of 18 schools across four continents, uniting young people from over 155 countries. Founded on the belief that education can bridge cultural divides, UWC empowers students to become agents of change through shared living, experiential learning, and service.

🏔️

UWC Dilijan, Armenia

Nestled in the lush forests of Dilijan National Park, UWC Dilijan opened in 2014 as the 14th UWC. Nearly 200 students from 80+ countries live, learn, and create together here. The campus blends world-class architecture with nature, fostering a vibrant community of thinkers, leaders, and changemakers.

🧋

What is Bolik?

Bolik is a student-founded CAS (Creativity, Activity, Service) initiative at UWC Dilijan. Born from a love for bubble tea and community, Bolik crafts premium drinks with hand-selected ingredients and delivers them across campus. Every cup supports student entrepreneurship and the spirit of service that defines UWC life.
